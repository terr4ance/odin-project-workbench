A Brief List Un-Lacking in Coherent Order

This is the order in which ingredients should be cooked.

  1. Firstly, preheat the oven as specified on your chips packet and put the chips on a baking tray ready to go in.
  2. Boil the kettle and prepare a measuring jug, a small saucepan, and a frying pan.
  3. Heat a small volume of oil or butter in a frying pan and add the sausages, turning occasionally.
  4. Put the chips in the oven and start the relevant timer.
  5. When the sausages and chips are almost done, put the peas in boiling water and place over the hob to cook through.
  6. Make gravy according to the manufacturer's instructions using the meausuring jug.
  7. Pile everything on a plate and there you go!
